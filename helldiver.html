Project Log & Status

Current Focus: Armor Assembly

Cutting and heat-forming the main armor plates from high-density EVA foam. The focus is on getting the shapes right before moving on to detailing.

First Steps: Helmet Construction

The project kicked off with 3D printing the base helmet. This will be reinforced and detailed to serve as the iconic centerpiece of the armor.
