Ogden Ortiz (Slime Rancher)

Wranglin' the tastiest, squirmiest slimes this side of the Moss Blanket.

Howdy! This page is dedicated to my Ogden Ortiz cosplay. The plan is to create his signature rancher outfit, build a prop vacpack, and, of course, sculpt some adorable Kookadoba-loving saber slimes. It's all about capturing the cozy, adventurous spirit of the Far, Far Range.
