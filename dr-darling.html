Dr. Darling (Control)

This is a Threshold. A place of discovery. Let's talk about Objects of Power.

Welcome to my research on Dr. Casper Darling. This project is about capturing the enthusiastic, slightly unhinged energy of the FBC's head of research. It involves sourcing the perfect lab coat, creating authentic-looking FBC documents, and maybe even building a prop or two from the labs.
