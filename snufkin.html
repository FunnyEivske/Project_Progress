Project Log & Status

Current Focus: The Hat

Searching for the right material to create Snufkin's iconic, floppy hat. The goal is to make it look well-traveled and loved, not brand new.

First Steps: The Green Coat

The project started by sourcing a durable, earthy green fabric for the coat. It needs to be comfortable enough for a long walk and have the right texture for weathering.
