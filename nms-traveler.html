Welcome, Traveler. This project is dedicated to building a full No Man's Sky exosuit. The goal is to capture the game's unique, retro-futuristic aesthetic, focusing on the helmet, the multi-tool, and the various life-support modules that make up the suit. Units received.

Project Log & Status

Current Focus: Multi-Tool Construction

Building the multi-tool prop from a combination of 3D printed parts and EVA foam. The next phase will be integrating electronics for the scanning light.

First Steps: Concept & Schematics

The journey began by gathering extensive in-game screenshots and concept art to create detailed blueprints for the exosuit and its components.
