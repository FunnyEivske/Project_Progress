Project V60-T

The ultimate dream: Merging Scandinavian design with American innovation.

This is the big one. The long-term goal is to take a Volvo V60 D6, a car I absolutely love, and perform a full electric vehicle conversion using a Tesla drivetrain and battery system. This project combines my passion for mechanics, electronics, and cutting-edge technology. It's an ambitious build that will require a ton of research, planning, and fabrication.

Project Log & Status

Current Focus: Drivetrain Research

Currently deep in the research phase, identifying the best Tesla motor and battery combination that will be compatible with the V60's chassis. This involves studying CAN bus systems, motor mounts, and power management.

First Steps: The Dream

The project began as a dream to combine the comfort and style of my favorite Volvo with the incredible performance of a Tesla. The first real step is saving up and sourcing the perfect V60 D6 to serve as the base.
