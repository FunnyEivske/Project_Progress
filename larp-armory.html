LARP Armory

Crafting battle-ready gear for adventures far and wide.

This section of the workshop is dedicated to designing and creating equipment for Live Action Role-Playing. From sturdy leather armor to safe foam weaponry, every piece is made with both aesthetics and practicality in mind. The ultimate goal is to build a complete kit for the big LARP event in Quebec, Canada!
