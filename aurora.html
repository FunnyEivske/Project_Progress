Aurora (VF)

Luring the shyest, most whispering hulders from the depths of the forest.

Hello! This page is dedicated to my Aurora cosplay from Villmarkens Forbannelse (The Curse of the Wilderness). The plan is to recreate her unique wilderness outfit, craft a mysterious prop, and, of course, sculpt some of the strange but fascinating creatures she surrounds herself with. The goal is to capture the enchanting, mystical, and nature-bound spirit from the deep, bewitched forests of Fredly.
