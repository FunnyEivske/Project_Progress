Aurora (VF)

Luring the shyest, most whispering hulders from the depths of the forest.

Hello! This page is dedicated to my Aurora cosplay from Villmarkens Forbannelse (The Curse of the Wilderness). The plan is to recreate her unique wilderness outfit, craft a mysterious prop, and, of course, sculpt some of the strange but fascinating creatures she surrounds herself with. The goal is to capture the enchanting, mystical, and nature-bound spirit from the deep, bewitched forests of Fredly.

Project Log & Status

Current Focus: Mysterious Forest Creature

Starting the design and material gathering for one of the strange creatures Aurora interacts with. The goal is to capture that enchanting but slightly unsettling vibe of the Fredly forests.

First Steps: The Outfit

The project is kicking off by researching and sourcing materials for Aurora's wilderness attire. I'm looking for fabrics and natural elements that feel earthy, mystical, and as if they are truly part of the forest itself.
